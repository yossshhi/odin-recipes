Bacon and Eggs Recipe

Home

Ingredients

  • 2 naan breads
  • 2 teaspoons olive oil, or as needed
  • 1/2 teaspoon garlic powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon dried dill
  • 2/3 cup tzatziki sauce
  • 1 cup shredded cooked chicken
  • 1/3 cup crumbled feta cheese
  • 1/4 small red onion, thinly sliced
  • 1/4 cup cherry tomatoes
  • 1/4 cup thinly sliced English cucumber

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 350 degrees F (180 degrees C).
  2. Place naan breads on a baking sheet; brush each with olive oil. Sprinkle evenly with garlic powder and dried dill. Top each naan with half of tzatziki sauce, 1/2 cup chicken, and half of feta cheese.
  3. Bake in the preheated oven until cheese is bubbly and the edges are nice and brown, about 7 minutes. Remove from the oven and top with red onion, tomatoes, and cucumber. Serve warm.
