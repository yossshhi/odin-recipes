Bacon and Eggs Recipe

Home

Ingredients

  • Bacon
  • 2 Eggs
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Butter
  • Long-lasting Milk
  • Bread

Steps

  1. Crack eggs and mix with milk to create egg batter
  2. Put bacon in pan, no oil needed.
  3. Fry bacon for 5-15 mins (depending on how crispy you want it)
  4. Toast bread
  5. After making bacon, immediately put egg batter in pan
